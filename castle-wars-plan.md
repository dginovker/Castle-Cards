Goal: Destroy enemy castle, collect points, unlock units

Two castles; you’re on left, enemy on right.  
Castle has 20 HP. You win when you destroy the enemy castle.  
Two unit modes: “Defend” and “Attack”  
You summon units for a cost of wood.  
You start with 20 wood and get 1 wood every 10 seconds.

Graphics: Something like that old Castle Wars game

| Unit | Stats | Mechanic |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Soldier | 1 Attack 3 Health Cost: 5 wood Attack range: 2 | Attack Mode: Unit marches forwards. Attacks anything near it Defend Mode: Runs to castle. Attacks anything near the player’s castle |
| Archer | 2 Attack 2 Health Cost: 5 wood Attack range: 10 | Attack Mode: Unit marches forwards, but if the player owns non-archer units, the archer stays behind those units. Shoots at enemies in range. Defend mode: Runs behind the castle. Shoots at enemies in range. |
| Drummer | 0 Attack 5 Health Cost: 10 wood  Attack range: 0 Leadership range: 4 | Attack Mode: Unit marches forward, but stays behind other units Defend mode: Runs behind the player’s castle Leadership: Gives \+50% attack to nearby units. |
| Statue | 0 Attack 0 Health Cost: 10 wood Attack range: 0 Leadership range: 5 | Attack Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. Defend Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. Leadership: \+50% attack to nearby units. Max: 1 |
| Well | 0 Attack 0 Health Cost: 10 wood Attack range: 0 Leadership range: 5 | Attack Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. Defend Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. Healing: Nearby units regain 1 health every 5 seconds Max: 1 |
| Tower | 1 Attack 0 Health Cost: 10 wood Attack range: 5 | Attack Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. Defend Mode: Is a structure. Stays inside the castle. |

Leadership mechanics:

* Different leaderships stack, but same leaderships do not; i.e. one Drummer gives nearby units \+50% damage, but two Drummers do not give units get \+100% damage. However, one statue gives \+50% damage, so a unit in range of Statue \+ Drummer leadership will have 100% more damage.

Future mechanics:

* Trees that grow \+ woodcutters  
  * Cutting wood makes the woodcutter slower, but then it comes back to your base and deposits the wood  
  * Killing enemy woodcutter gives you the wood
